My Coding Journey

A few months ago, I decided to learn coding because I was curious about how websites and applications are made. In the beginning, I felt enthusiastic and motivated to learn many new things. Every day, I watched tutorials and practiced writing simple codes on my computer.

Unfortunately, learning coding was not as easy as I imagined. Sometimes, the program I created showed many errors that I did not understand. I often felt confused because even a small mistake could make the whole program fail. There were nights when I spent a long time fixing bugs but still could not find the solution.

Because of those difficulties, I sometimes felt stressed and wanted to stop learning coding. I thought that maybe coding was not the right thing for me. However, I remembered my goal and tried to stay patient. I continued practicing little by little and learned from my mistakes.

After working hard and never giving up, I finally succeeded in completing my coding project. The experience taught me that failure is a normal part of learning. Although coding can be difficult and frustrating, persistence and effort will eventually lead to success.
